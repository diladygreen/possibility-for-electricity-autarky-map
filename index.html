Possibility for renewable electricity autarky in Europe

This map visualises the possibility for renewable electricity autarky in Europe --- for all municipalities, all regions, all countries, and all of Europe. Having the possibility means being able to fulfill local electricity demand with 100% renewable electricity from wind and solar power generated locally.

Areas wont be able to become autarkic when their local electricity demand exceeds the technical potential of renewable electricity generation. Technical potential is based on technical restrictions only, and the actually feasible potential might be much lower than what we assume here, for example because local administration does not want to use all available land for electricity generation. Hence we categorise the areas in the following way:

  • Autarky is impossible (dark red), when demand exceeds technical potential.
  • Autarky is likely impossible (light red), when technical potential is less than twice the demand.
  • Autarky might be possible (light green), when technical potential is less than 10 times the demand.
  • Autarky is likely possible (dark green), when technical potential is more than 10 times the demand.

The data is taken from the peer-reviewed article: Tim Tröndle, Stefan Pfenninger, and Johan Lilliestam (in review). Home-made or imported: on the possibility for renewable electricity autarky on all scales in Europe. Energy Strategy Reviews. If you make use of the data in academic work, please cite the article.
